Experimental Typography
(18 x 27 inch)
Fall 2019, Typography 1
This poster is made by combining typographic elements obtained through a total of six exercises. In general, letters in the contrasting words ‘wide’ and ‘narrow’, other related words, and even related sentences and phrases were used. Rather than content delivery or topical analysis, the focus was on layout and alignment, but mostly on hierarchy. It is also intended to illustrate hierarchy well and attract others’ attention on specific parts when it is first encountered. Furthermore, the paragraphs were used as a design component to provide detail to an appropriate place.
