Sea Sweet (self-promotion item)
(1.8 x 2.5 inch)
2019
This project was to make something that can be used as a self-promotion item. Two themes are combined that represent me well together; dessert and the sea. These are the things where I get lots of inspiration from when I work. Shapes are inspired by sea creatures and the color of the desserts, and are made with acrylic paint. Items are all hand-made since I wanted my items to be unique and original. Nonetheless, because the main purpose of self-promotion items is to distribute them to many people and inform me, my information should be written and mass-production should have been possible. Thus, after moving the handmade work into a graphic, I designed a keychain which is a surely portable and familiar object.
