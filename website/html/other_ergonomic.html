Ergonomic
(35.5 x 47 x 23 inch)
2018
This project was about ‘ergonomic’. For this project, I decided to make something wearable and has a causal relationship to the body. So, I embodied the natural yet flexing posture ‘crouching pose’. At first glance, gloves, socks, and string on the neck may look separate, but in fact, they are a piece of work linked together. Therefore, it is impossible to only wear one part of the work to complete the performance. When one starts to insert the feet into the work, it naturally leads to the next move. To wrap it up, it is designed to suit the movement that the human body can take. On top of that, details were added with beads symbolizing tears to match the posture of crouching.
