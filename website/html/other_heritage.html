Heritage
(48 x 18 inch)
2019
This project came from the subject of ‘Heritage’. I interpreted the word heritage as meaning ‘something passed on by the past generation’ and drew a circumstance of a person handing something over to another. Moreover, I thought the flow that is being transmitted seems calm on the outside but actually goes through a tremendous process. Thus I symbolized the process as noise and again visualized it as complex and quirky patterns. Also, I depicted door and mirror to give a feeling of repetition. In the drawing, we can feel as if we are in a room of mirrors, stripped of our ability to sense time and space.
