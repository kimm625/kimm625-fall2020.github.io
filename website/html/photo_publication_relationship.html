(8 x 11.5 inch)
2019, Personal Project
Pottery is just a product we see every day. The ceramics in this booklet do not matter whether it was made by a particular author or not. Ordinary objects of everyday life were created with a new idea of object by new purpose and vision, abandoning their practical qualities. Also in the booklet, ceramics become art pieces that deviate from normal context, and pottery laid carelessly on someone’s house shelf will one day just remain a burden. I wanted to give artistry to the pottery that was being sold to the world in a normal way by making a ‘choice’. The ceramics provided as a ready-made in the work are combined with the human body and lose their usual function and name. In other words, the relationship between objects and names, created by arbitrary rules and habits, is born anew as an object outside of verbal possession. It is a personal taste to decide whether the so-called discovered object is beautiful or unusual.
