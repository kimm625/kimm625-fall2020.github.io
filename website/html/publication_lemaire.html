Brand translation
(6 x 6 inch)
2019
This work is a study of the visual storytelling of a brand in a sequence. The brand I decided to use is a French high fashion brand called ‘Lemaire’. And throughout the accordion book, it’s storytelling is changing from left to right; from an image of f/w season to s/s season. To start the project, I gathered the words that best describe the brand which was ‘silhouette, simple, neutral colors, elegance’. It is showing the change of seasons by a mix match of colors and patterns that contains the mood of the brand. Moreover, the silhouette was a crucial element of Lemaire, so I worked only with various amount of geometric shapes using vectors and lots of details. Putting a red line through the whole accordion book without splashing, the change was made more naturally. The same effect had been applied with the logos on the front and back. And I also created gif animation in response to the project.
