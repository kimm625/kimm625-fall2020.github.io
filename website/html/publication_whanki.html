Storytelling Through Visualization
(8.3 x 11.7 inch)
2020
For this project, I built a narrative using infographics and visualizations to reinterpret a story of my choice, which is a book called “Paperback”. This book contains letters from historical painter Whanki Kim with his eternal muse, wife, that they exchanged over Seoul, Paris, and New York. Beyond simply organizing the storyline of the book, diverse data was analyzed, the flow of the story was grasped and expressed visually. I reinterpreted the delicate and emotional feelings contained in the letters in my own sensibility through the colors and the organic shape of graphs.
