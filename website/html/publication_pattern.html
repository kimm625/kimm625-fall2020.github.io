Pattern book
(6 x 8 inch)
2019
This project was to make the book filled with patterns that fit the theme. My theme was 'Traditional Asian pottery', and rather than bring shapes as they are, I reinterpreted them into modern shapes. Despite the transformation, I attempted not to lose the elegance and peace that pottery has. So the shapes in the booklet are mostly made up of curves and earthy colors. Each page contains different styles of patterns.
