Photographic Autobiography
(8.5 x 11 inch)
2019
This project visualizes an autobiographical narrative. I explored and considered how visual images expressed through photographs can synergize with texts. The context for the design is a small-scale publication. The cover introduces the narrative arc of the project which indicates my delicacy, using an egg as a metaphor. The initial atmosphere of the book is dizzy, twisted, and it even shows me wandering not knowing where to go. When it gets to the back, I, myself become settled and eventually get free from restraint. The booklet ends with a portrait of me being happy for finding the right path.
