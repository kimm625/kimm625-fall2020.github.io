Wiki Book (2000)
(6 x 9 inch)
2019
Using Wikipedia’s own list of longest pages, I chose a theme of ‘2000’ which contains all the important occasions that happened throughout the decade. This was a larger typographic project that applies all of the elements I learned so far, such as, typographic layout and visual rhythm applied to several pages (grid) and the concept of style. In addition to designing a printed book, I extended my piece to a screen design. The book includes 176 pages with the interview and 11contents.
