Evidence
2019
This is a work that took place in a photography class and is handling the story of absence and existence. To explain, often only after someone has left, we recognize oneâ€™s existence. After all, the absence is a testament to its presence. The work shows how records of absence gather to prove that it exists. I photographed various marks I found wandering around and printed them in diverse ways. Such as, inserting text into pictures, collaging photos to the documents that support the topic, and printing them on different kinds of paper. To show my intention more clearly, I put all the elements of the work into a file that seems to be an evidence file.
