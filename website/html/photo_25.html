The Photographer’s Eye
2019
This assignment identifies and explores five essential characteristics of a photograph as defined by John Szarkowski:
1) the thing itself 2) the detail 3) the frame 4) time 5) vantage point
I concentrated on varying the way I interpret each of the five characteristicts and responded by shooting photographs which explore each of these five elements. All photos are taken in Baltimore, MD, USA.
