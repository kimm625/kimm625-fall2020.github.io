Liquid Modernity
Fall 2019, Typography 1
Through this poster, the theme I want to convey is the modern era that does not sit still and changes countless times like a liquid. Thus, I organized the side effects of technological advances and increased online contacts into four and searched for the proper solution. The contents are simply compressed and the empty space on the poster was utilized properly. Furthermore, the two most essential and contrasting words which represent the problem and solution of the topic were ‘over-connected’ and ‘silence’, so the words itself and letters o and s are being repeated as decoration. The poster has an elegant, classic mood using the right typefaces and colors so that the work can have a calm and earnest feeling.
