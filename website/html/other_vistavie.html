Mantra Merch
2020
For this project I chose my personal mantra ‘vis ta vie’ (from the movie 'Attila Mercel') which means ‘Live Your Life’ in French. Then I designed the logo out of it and produced an actual merchandise item. The logo was intended to give a feeling of silence and serenity just like grandmother advising to their grandchild gently while having a cup of tea.
