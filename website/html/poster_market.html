Traditional Marketplace
(18 x 24 inch)
Spring 2020, Typography 2
This project was to select a place and produce a poster listing its 10 characteristics (literal) and a poster with its feelings (expressive). I chose the ‘Traditional Market’ as my place and was inspired by the lights and map of the night market. As an extension of the poster, the iPad screen design, and the screens for Instagram were also designed.
In screen design, the initial ‘M’ from the word market was used as a metaphor for the gate, and an egg-looking shape was used for the contents page. The paragraphs on the detailed page are contained in a map market looking from the top, and natural graphics representing the free of the traditional market are included in the background.
