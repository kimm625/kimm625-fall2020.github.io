Manifesto Poster
(578 x 380 mm)
2020
For this project I used a historical manifesto from company Apple: Here’s to the Crazy Ones; and created two typographic posters. First poster contains the whole manifesto words, and second poster is designed to announce a lecture. When combining words, I tried to put rational and formal mood that the original manifesto has. At the same time I put some craziness which aims the ‘crazy ones.’
